The Problem:

allow busy people to order healthy food choices for their family & allow foodies to experience new flavors wherever they go.

The Goal:

The goal of the project is to allow busy people to order food new & great tasting foods.

Target Audience

Busy people that wanted to order something quick & healthy.

Project Constraints

During this project there were several constraints to deal with

Meal options

James is upset because there aren't very many healthy options that he or his wife can give their family.

Delivery

James is upset because where he lives there aren't very many places that deliver to his address.

Tiredness

James & his wife are sometimes too tired after their day to cook or prepare meals.

Ingredients

James wants to see which ingredients are available so he can make an informed decision for his family.
